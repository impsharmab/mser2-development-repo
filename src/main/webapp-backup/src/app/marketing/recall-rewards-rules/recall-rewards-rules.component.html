Recall Rewards

N45 and N46

 

Eligible Participants:

MSER Enrolled Service Advisors and their MSER enrolled Service Manager

 

Program Launch Date:

August 24, 2015

 

Program Details:

Service Advisors enrolled in Mopar Service Excellence Rewards (MSER) will earn $10 for each warranty repair order submitted through qualifying DMS systems (see MSER Program Rules) with the following part numbers and op codes.

 

Part Numbers: LOP's:
CBXTN451AA 13-N4-51-82
CBXTN461AA 13-N4-51-83
  13-N4-61-82
  13-N4-61-83
  13-N4-61-53

 

LOPs indicating inspection was completed but no repair/part required:

13-N4-51-81

13-N4-61-81

 

Service Managers will qualify for the 10% override on the Service Advisors reward.

 

Qualified ROs and rewards earned will be reported on the MSER website www.moparser.com. Rewards will be paid on the Rewarding Excellence® Visa® Prepaid Card, according to the schedule posted on the MSER website.
